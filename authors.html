Information for Authors

The SU-ICEIT conference, scheduled for 2026, serves as the leading event for engineering and technology in the Kurdistan Region of Iraq. Hosted and organized by the College of Engineering at Salahaddin University-Erbil, the conference has gained recognition for publishing papers online in Salahaddin Conference Proceedings.

Conference Overview

  • Name: International Conference on Engineering and Technology (SU-ICEIT)
  • Year: 2026
  • Hosted By: College of Engineering at Salahaddin University-Erbil
  • Location: Erbil, Kurdistan Region of Iraq
  • Prospective Publication of Papers: Papers that meet the conference requirements will be published online in Salahaddin Conference Proceedings.

Terms and Conditions

  • Language: English
  • Article Length: 5-20 pages inclusive
  • Number of Papers: Each author can present a maximum of two papers. Additional papers require registration of accompanying co-author(s).
  • Registration: Authors must register for the conference and present their work.
  • No Show Policy: Papers will be published only if at least one author is registered and the paper is presented. Non-presented papers may be excluded from online distribution post-conference.
  • Authorization to Publish: Authors must ensure that their material is available for general dissemination. Signed SU-ICEIT2026 Copyright Forms are required for all accepted papers.

Paper Format and Submission

  • Paper Format:

    To make the submission process smoother, we kindly request that authors follow these instructions.

    Please prepare your manuscript in MS-Word using Times New Roman font with a size of 12 pt and adhere to the Harvard style for referencing.

    When drafting your paper, focus solely on the content and structure, as our conference and journal team will handle the formatting.

    Below, you'll find the recommended order for organizing your paper.

    Thank you for your cooperation in maintaining consistency and efficiency throughout the submission process.The manuscript should be compiled in the following order:

    Title, should include: Title, Authors and affiliations, Corresponding author

    Abstract

    Introduction

    Main Content

    Conclusion

    Acknowledgement

    Conflict of Interest

    References (Harvard Style only)

    Tables: Refer to Tables in the text as Table 1, etc. Use Table 1 (boldface), etc., in the title of the Table.

    Figures: The number of figures should be no more than 6 and appropriate for the presented data.

    Figures should be referred to as Figure 1, Figures 2, 3-5 (boldface), using Arabic numerals.

    Submission

    Once your article is prepared, please utilize the provided link in Submission Section to submit the paper. You will receive an automated email confirming the successful submission. Subsequently, within a month's time, you can anticipate receiving another email regarding the acceptance for presentation at the conference, potential revisions, or the rejection of the paper.
