Call for Papers

SU-ICEIT’2026, organized by the College of Engineering at Salahaddin University-Erbil, stands as the flagship conference for engineering and technology in the Kurdistan region of Iraq, held biennially. Covering a broad spectrum, the conference addresses topics across various engineering and science disciplines, including but not limited to

  • Architecture Engineering
  • Civil and Structural Engineering
  • Building Materials Engineering
  • Geotechnical Engineering
  • Transportation Engineering
  • Geomatics and Survey Engineering
  • Electrical and Computer Engineering
  • Power and Renewable Energy Systems Engineering
  • Automation, Control, Robotics Engineering
  • Communication Engineering
  • Electronics Engineering
  • Photonics and Optoelectronics Engineering
  • Industrial Engineering
  • Aerospace Engineering
  • Biomedical Engineering
  • Software Engineering
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Internet of Things Engineering
  • Cybersecurity Engineering
  • Water Resources Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Sustainability Engineering
  • Petroleum and Petrochemical Engineering
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Mechanical and Automotive Engineering
  • Production and Materials Engineering
  • Data Science and Engineering
  • Big Data and Cloud Computing
  • Engineering Management
  • Risk and Reliability Engineering
  • Information Technology and Computer Science
  • Machine Learning Applications in Engineering
  • Humanitarian Engineering

Paper Formatting and Submission Guidelines

Papers must be from 5-20 pages inclusive, please refer to the For Authors or Submission sections for guidelines on paper formatting and submission.
